ABOUT

Welcome to koko, where people celebrate the experience of life and gifting in a very special, delicious setting. We are proud of our company for providing our customers with high quality products and a friendly customer experience. Feel free to browse our website, shop online, or stop by our factory the next time you are in northern Massachusetts. We hope you enjoy our finest quality collections and tasty recipes.

Our History

Khloe "koko" Appleseed, our founder, started koko in December of 1997. She made Ice Cream, handled shipping and receiving, local deliveries and assisting in chocolate molding. koko is a lot busier now that there are over 100 stores. Nonetheless, Khloe still feels the same joy as when she started the business when she meets new customers or develops new products.

Our Mission

koko is committed to responsible corporate citizenship. We strive to actively participate in local charitable events with both tangible and intangible gifting. Our core purpose and values define and guides our daily behaviors and practices. It is this culture that brings our goals to life each day, extended to our customers and local community.
